Overview

Legal invoice review is a critical component to the overall outside counsel and spend management process. When legal invoice review is appropriately combined with the power of eBilling, the benefits can be substantial. Mary O'Carroll (Head of Legal Operations at Google) discusses the journey that Google took to study, implement, and eventually outsource their legal bill review program.
